Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 8, 2025

Access Auto Glass ("we," "our," "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you request a quote, call us, or use our website.

Information We Collect

When you submit a quote request or contact us, we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Service location
  • Vehicle details (year, make, model, VIN)
  • Uploaded images of vehicle damage

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your quote requests
  • Schedule and provide services
  • Communicate with you regarding your service
  • Improve our services and website

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share it with trusted service providers solely for the purpose of fulfilling your request.

Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your personal information, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at accessautoglass323@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
